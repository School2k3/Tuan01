A smile is a facial expression formed primarily by flexing the muscles at the sides of the mounth. It is believed it takes about 80 muscles to smile. some smiles include a contraction of the muscles at the corner of the eyes, an action known as a "Duchenne smile". Smiles performed without the eye contraction may be perceived as insincere.
